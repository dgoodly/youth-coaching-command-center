# 3-Day Athletic Development Split — Reference Program

> A/S-tier reference program at 3-day frequency (Tue · Wed · Fri). Companion to
> `4Day_Athletic_Split_Program.md`; same chassis, one fewer session. Referenced by
> `BUILD_BRIEF.md` as a reference shape for assembled 3-day sessions and an anchor for
> movement-library tagging. Governed by `COACHING_INSTRUCTIONS.md` (research-revised).

## How it works
Sprint / Jump / Lift chassis. Within every session: **Jump → Sprint → Lift** (highest-CNS
work first; strength closes). Dose scaled to biological maturity (low loads, technique-first,
full recoveries, progress by adding quality reps/exposures — not load or stopwatch, and not
by extending flat-out distance).

**Hard rule:** max-velocity sprint quality is protected — never downstream of a HEAVY plyo
block. Day 1 (Tue) keeps the runs fresh; Days 2 and 3 carry NO max-velocity. A small, sharp
plyo primer *before* the runs is fine (it potentiates) — the rule bars heavy plyo VOLUME
upstream, not all jumping.

**Sprint note (research-revised):** max-velocity uses FLYING-SPRINT structure — a short
build-up into a short maximal fly zone (~10–30 yd) — NOT long flat-out 40–60+ yd runs, which
spike eccentric hamstring load. Ramp high-speed volume gradually (~5%/week).

### Weekly map
| Day | Session | Sprint Emphasis | Plyo Density | Warm-Up Funnel |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 (Tue) | Max Velocity + Lower | Max-V (fresh, flying) | Low (prime only) | Linear Speed |
| 2 (Wed) | Jump Quality + Upper | Short accel only | Moderate | Linear Speed (short) |
| 3 (Fri) | Explosive / Lateral-Rotational + Accel + Posterior | Accel + COD | High | Change of Direction |

Mon/Thu/weekend off. Slot sport sessions into the gaps. On any day with a game/practice, cut
training volume ~30%. Sleep 9–10 hrs. Track standing + sitting height quarterly and ease the
dose through the peak-height-velocity window.

## The Warm-Up System — Base + Funnel
Every session = shared BASE + day-specific FUNNEL.
**Funnel by day:** Day 1 → Linear Speed · Day 2 → Linear Speed (short) · Day 3 → Change of Direction.

### BASE LAYER — Daily Dynamic (every session, ~8 min)
Each locomotion drill ~10 yards; one pass each.
**Locomotion:** high-knee tuck · high-knee run · hurdler walk · butt-kick run · figure-4 walk ·
straight-leg march · walking lunge · reverse lunge · lateral lunge · dynamic waiter's bow.
**Ground:** prisoner squats ×10 · overhead toe touch ×5/side · fire hydrants 5 paused + 5 fluid/side ·
hip circles 5 paused + 5 fluid/side.

### FUNNEL A — Linear Speed (Days 1 & 2)
Day 1 full; Day 2 short (first three drills). SL foot isolations (ground absorption) → power
skip for height → horizontal skip → A-skip → B-skip → power B-skip → straight-leg bounds.
On Day 1 keep skip/bound volume light — prime, don't pre-fatigue.

### FUNNEL B — Change of Direction (Day 3)
Doubles as knee-tracking prep. Bilateral lateral shuffle → SL lateral movement → line hops →
lateral power shuffle → karaoke (power + speed) → backward run → backpedal. Knee tracks OUT
over the 2nd–3rd toe on every push/plant.

## DAY 1 — Max Velocity + Lower Strength
*Warm-up: BASE + Linear Speed (full). Freshest day — speed is the priority; jumps prime only.*

**JUMP — prime only (low volume; potentiates the runs)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Linear pogo | 2 × 10 | 30s | Stiff ankles, quick ground contact |
| SL pogo + stick | 2 × 5 / leg | 45s | Knee stacked over the toes, no cave |
| Low hurdle hop (2-foot) | 2 × 4 | 60s | Get off the ground fast, soft knees |

**SPRINT — Max Velocity (flying-sprint structure)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Distance | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Build-ups (ramp to ~90%) | 3 | 30 yd | walk-back | Smooth acceleration, tall finish |
| Flying sprint (build → max fly @ 95–100%) | 4–5 | 20 yd build → 20 yd fly | ~3 min | All-out through the fly zone; run tall |

*End on velocity/quality drop-off, not accumulated distance. No 40–60 yd flat-out runs.*

**LIFT — Lower**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Back squat (or trap-bar) | 4 × 4 | 2–3 min | Knees track out; controlled descent |
| RLE split squat (DB) | 3 × 6 / leg | 90s | Vertical shin, knee tracks the toe |
| Hip thrust | 3 × 8 | 90s | Drive hips, squeeze at the top |

**TRUNK — rotation / anti-rotation**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest |
|---|---|---|
| MB rotational throw (wall) | 3 × 6 / side | 45s |
| Pallof press | 3 × 8 / side | 45s |

*Cooldown (5 min): hip flexor + hamstring + calf, slow breathing.*

## DAY 2 — Jump Quality + Upper Strength
*Warm-up: BASE + Linear Speed (short). Day after max-V — no max-velocity today. Includes motor-skill breadth.*

**JUMP — quality / contrast (all planes + SL stick)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Vertical jump + stick | 3 × 4 | 75s | Extend fully, land quiet, knees out |
| Broad jump (non-consecutive, stick) | 3 × 3 | 75s | Jump far, land soft, freeze |
| Lateral bound + stick | 3 × 4 / side | 60s | Push wide, stick and hold — no cave |
| 90° rotational hop + stick | 3 × 4 / side | 60s | Turn in the air, land balanced |

**SPRINT — short acceleration only**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Distance | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Ground start → sprint | 4 | 10–15 yd | 90s | Push the ground away, drive out low |
| Resisted sprint (heavy sled) | 4 | 10 yd | 90s | Stay low, punch the ground back |

**MOTOR-SKILL — general movement breadth**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps / Time | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| MB overhead + rotational throw-catch | 3 × 6 / side | 45s | Power from the hips, turn through |
| Reactive ball-drop catch / coordination | 2–3 min | — | React fast, clean hands |

**LIFT — Upper**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bench / DB floor press | 4 × 5 | 2 min | Smooth and controlled both ways |
| Neutral-grip pull-up (volume) | 4 × sub-max | 90s | Chase monthly total reps |
| 1-arm DB row | 3 × 8 / side | 75s | Elbow to hip, no twist |
| Hand-release push-up (4 s eccentric) | 2 × 8 | 60s | Slow down-phase, hands off at bottom |

*Cooldown (5 min): lat + pec stretch, t-spine rotations.*

## DAY 3 — Explosive / Lateral-Rotational Power + Acceleration + Posterior
*Warm-up: BASE + Change of Direction (full). Fresh after Thursday off — highest plyo density + CoD + posterior chain.*

**JUMP — power density (depth drops + contrast, all planes + SL)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Depth drop (stick + hold) → hurdle hops | 3 × (1 drop + 3 hops) | 90s | Absorb soft, then attack the hurdles |
| Hurdle hop → box jump (contrast) | 3 × (3 + 1) | 90s | Low-quick into one big explosion |
| SL bounds (consecutive) | 3 × 4 / leg | 90s | Big scissor, claw the ground back |
| Lateral hurdle hop → lateral box jump | 3 × (3 + 1) / side | 90s | Quick sideways into one lateral push |

**SPRINT — Acceleration + Change of Direction (not max-V)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Distance | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Half-kneeling / 3-point start → sprint | 4 | 15 yd | 90s | Fire out flat and low |
| 5-10-5 shuttle | 4 | — | 2 min | Chop feet, sit hips, knee out on plant |
| Reactive cone cuts (coach calls) | 4–5 | — | 75s | React; sharp plant, explode out |

**LIFT — Posterior / Total**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Distance | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Trap-bar deadlift (or heavy hinge) | 4 × 4 | — | 2–3 min | Flat back, drive the floor away |
| Nordic curl (control the eccentric) | 3 × 5 | — | 90s | Fight gravity the whole way down |
| Heavy sled march (high-handle) | 3 | 20–30 yd | 75s | Biggest step, drive through |
| SL squat / pistol to box | 3 × 5 / leg | — | 90s | Control down, knee tracks the toe |

**TRUNK — anti-rotation / anti-lateral**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Distance | Rest |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tall-kneeling Pallof press | 3 × 10 / side | — | 45s |
| Suitcase carry | 2 | 20 yd / side | 60s |

*Cooldown (5 min): couch stretch, hamstring, calves.*

## Design notes
- **Two speed exposures / week, ≥48h apart:** Day 1 (max-V) and Day 3 (accel + CoD). Day 2's
  short accel is low-CNS and doesn't count as a third quality speed day.
- **Hamstring load split:** max-V (Tue) and Nordics + heavy hinge (Fri) are on separate days,
  not stacked.
- **Valgus / landing woven in:** base warm-up hip work, the CoD funnel (Day 3), the stick on
  every landing, frontal-plane strength, and single-leg bias. Stop any set when knees cave.
- **Motor-skill breadth:** Day 2 throw-catch + reactive coordination (lighter for S-tier, but present).

## Testing & progress markers (re-test every 3–4 weeks)
10- & 20-yd dash (acceleration) · flying-10 (top-end) · broad jump (horizontal power) · SL hop
distance each leg (SL power + valgus screen — watch side-to-side gaps) · drop-jump landing
screen. Frame as "beat your old number." Progress by adding quality; load creeps up only when
technique is airtight.

## Tier note
This is the **A/S-tier reference.** Lower tiers run the same skeleton with regressed movements
(double-leg landings, no depth drops, acceleration-only sprinting, bodyweight/iso strength,
lower plyo density). See `Youth_Tiering_Assessment_Spec.md` §5 and the assembler's tier gating.
