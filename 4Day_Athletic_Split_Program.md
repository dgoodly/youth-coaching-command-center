# 4-Day Athletic Development Split — Reference Program

> The fully built A/S-tier reference program (experienced 9-year-old; football /
> basketball / track; in-season, light load; knee-valgus watch). Referenced by
> `BUILD_BRIEF.md` as (a) the shape an assembled session should resemble and (b) the
> seed for the movement library's tier tagging. Governed by `COACHING_INSTRUCTIONS.md`.

## How it works
Sprint / Jump / Lift chassis. Within every session: **Jump → Sprint → Lift** (highest-CNS
work first; strength closes). Dose scaled for a pre-pubertal 9-year-old (low loads,
technique-first, full recoveries, progress by adding reps/distance, not load or stopwatch).

**Hard rule:** max-velocity sprint quality is protected — never downstream of a heavy plyo
block. Day 1 keeps the runs fresh; Days 2 and 4 carry NO max-velocity work.

### Weekly map
| Day | Session | Sprint Emphasis | Plyo Density | Warm-Up Funnel |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 (Mon) | Max Velocity + Lower | Max-V (fresh) | Low | Linear Speed |
| 2 (Tue) | Plyo Density / SL + Upper | Short accel only | High | Linear Speed (short) |
| 3 (Thu) | Accel / Multidir + Posterior | Accel + COD | Moderate | Change of Direction |
| 4 (Fri) | Lateral & Rotational + Upper | Reactive only | High | Change of Direction |

Wednesday + weekend off. Slot 0–2 weekly sport sessions into the gaps. On any day with a
game/practice, cut training volume ~30%. Sleep 9–10 hrs.

## The Warm-Up System — Base + Funnel

Every session = shared BASE + day-specific FUNNEL.
**Funnel by day:** Day 1 → Linear Speed · Day 2 → Linear Speed (short) · Day 3 → Change of
Direction · Day 4 → Change of Direction.

### BASE LAYER — Daily Dynamic (every session, ~8 min)
Each locomotion drill ~10 yards; one pass each (scaled for a 9-year-old).

**Locomotion series:** high-knee tuck (walking) · high-knee run · hurdler walk (quad
stretch) · butt-kick run · figure-4 walk · straight-leg march · walking lunge · reverse
lunge · lateral lunge · dynamic waiter's bow.

**Ground movement:** prisoner squats ×10 · overhead toe touch ×5 each side · fire hydrants
5 pause + 5 fluid each · hip circles 5 pause + 5 fluid each.

*Valgus built in: lateral lunge, fire hydrants, hip circles wake the glutes/abductors that
control knee tracking.*

### FUNNEL A — Linear Speed (Days 1 & 2, ~5–6 min)
Day 1 full; Day 2 short (first three drills only — the plyo block handles the rest). On
Day 1 keep skip/bound volume LIGHT (prime, don't pre-fatigue).

| Drill | Dose | Cue |
|---|---|---|
| SL foot isolations (ground absorption) | 2 × 5 each foot | Absorb quietly — minimal bounce |
| Power skip for height | 2 × 10 yd | Punch the knee up, drive vertical, stay tall |
| Horizontal/distance skip | 2 × 20 yd | Push forward, cover ground |
| A-skip | 2 × 15 yd | Tall torso, clean arms, foot lands under hip |
| B-skip | 2 × 15 yd | Thigh ~90°, paw straight down, ankle flexed |
| Power B-skip | 1 × 15 yd | More force through the down leg |
| Straight-leg bounds | 2 × 15 yd | Long stride, soft knee, claw the ground back |

*Day 2 short version: SL foot isolations → power skip for height → horizontal skip.*

### FUNNEL B — Change of Direction (Days 3 & 4, ~5–6 min)
Targets foot inversion/eversion, knee stability, hip abduction/adduction. **This funnel is
the day's primary valgus work** — trains knee tracking right before cutting stress.

| Drill | Dose | Cue |
|---|---|---|
| Bilateral lateral shuffle | 2 × 10 yd ea way | Low athletic stance, don't cross the feet |
| Single-leg lateral movement | 2 × 8 yd each | Balance on one leg — foot/ankle control |
| Line hops (lateral + fwd/back) | 2 × 15 sec | Quick foot contacts, light and reactive |
| Lateral power shuffle | 3 × each way | Sit into hips, push off OUTSIDE leg, wide + low |
| Karaoke — power | 2 × 15 yd ea | Big bounds, full range, rotate through hips |
| Karaoke — speed | 2 × 15 yd ea | Quick high knees, torso upright |
| Backward run | 2 × 15 yd | Kinesthetic awareness, quick controlled steps |
| Backpedal | 2 × 15 yd | Restore balance/symmetry, stay low |

*Knee-tracking cue throughout: knee tracks OUT over the 2nd–3rd toe on every push/plant.*

## DAY 1 — Max Velocity + Lower Strength
*Warm-up: BASE + Linear Speed (full). PRIORITY: SPRINT — keep it fresh; jump block primes only.*

**JUMP — prime only (low density)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| SL pogo + stick | 2 × 6 each | 45s | Land and freeze — knee stacked over toes, no cave |
| SL stick box jump (2-leg jump, 1-leg land) | 3 × 4 each | 60s | Stick it silent. Knee out over 2nd–3rd toe |

**SPRINT — Max Velocity (the priority)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Build-ups (ramp to ~90%) | 3 × 30 yd | walk back | Smooth acceleration, tall finish |
| Flying sprints (15 yd build → 20 yd fly) | 4–5 reps | 2–3 min | All-out through the zone. Run tall and fast |

*Progress: 4→5 reps, or fly 20→25. Add reps/distance, never chase the clock.*

**LIFT — Lower (eccentric / iso emphasis)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Goblet squat | 3 × 6–8 | 75s | Proud chest. Knees track OUT, not in |
| Decel step-up | 3 × 6 each | 60s | Pockets straight down, kiss the heel. Own the lowering |
| Nordic / buddy curl | 3 × 5 | 75s | Control the way down |
| Cossack squat | 3 × 6 each | 60s | Sit into one hip, knee tracks the toe |
| Pallof press (anti-rotation) | 3 × 8 each | 45s | Resist the twist — brace, don't bend |

*Cooldown (5 min): hip flexor + hamstring + calf, slow breathing.*

## DAY 2 — Plyo Density / Single-Leg + Upper
*Warm-up: BASE + Linear Speed (short). NO MAX-V (downstream of the plyo block). Short accel only.*
*Stop any plyo set the moment the knees start to cave — a clean set of 4 beats a sloppy 8.*

**JUMP — contrast chains + single-leg (high density)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pogo prep (linear → lateral) | 2 × 10 | 30s | Ground-up, quick and stiff |
| Hurdle hop → box jump (contrast) | 3 × (3 hops + 1 jump) | 75s | Low-quick into one big explosion |
| SL hops, non-consecutive (stick each) | 3 × 4 each | 60s | Hop, stick, reset. Knee out every landing |
| Skater bounds (stick) | 3 × 5 each | 60s | Push wide off outside foot, stick and hold |
| Depth drop (stick + hold) → hurdle hops | 3 × (1 drop + 3 hops) | 75s | Absorb soft and silent, then attack hurdles |

**SPRINT — short acceleration only**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ground starts → 10 yd | 4 × 10 yd | 90s | Push the ground away — drive out like diving into a pool |
| Resisted sprint (light–mod sled) | 4 × 10 yd | 90s | Effort drill. Stay low, punch the ground back |

**LIFT — Upper (pull-up chased for volume)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Neutral-grip pull-up | 4 sets, sub-max | 90s | Dead hang to chin-over. Chase monthly total |
| Hand-release push-up (4s eccentric) | 3 × 6–8 | 60s | Elbows in, slow down-phase, hands off the floor |
| 1-arm DB row | 3 × 8 each | 60s | Drive elbow to hip, no twist |
| MB rotational throw (wall) | 3 × 6 each | 45s | Power from the hips — turn through the ball |

*Cooldown (5 min): lat + pec stretch, t-spine rotations.*

---

## DAY 3 — Acceleration / Multidirectional + Posterior
*Warm-up: BASE + Change-of-Direction (full). The CoD funnel is today's primary knee-tracking work.*

**JUMP — horizontal + frontal**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pogo prep (fwd / back / lateral) | 2 × 8 each | 30s | Stiff and reactive in every direction |
| Broad jump, non-consecutive (stick) | 3 × 4 | 75s | Jump far, land soft, knees out, freeze |
| Lateral hurdle hop → lateral box jump | 3 × (3 + 1) each | 75s | Quick sideways into one big lateral push |
| Bounds, consecutive | 3 × 5 each | 60s | Big scissor, claw the ground behind you |

**SPRINT — acceleration + change of direction**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Half-kneeling starts → 12 yd | 4 × 12 yd | 90s | Knee in line with toe, fire out flat and low |
| Lateral shuffle → sprint | 3 × each way | 90s | Plant the outside foot, redirect, go |
| 5-10-5 shuttle | 3–4 reps | 2 min | Chop the feet, sit the hips, knee out on the plant |

**LIFT — Posterior / Lower (sled staple)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Light barbell front squat (or goblet) | 3 × 6 | 90s | Upright torso, elbows high, controlled |
| DB / KB RDL (hinge) | 3 × 8 | 75s | Push hips back, flat back, feel the hamstrings |
| Heavy sled: high-handle march | 3 × 20–30 yd | 75s | Biggest step possible — drive and step through |
| Heavy sled: backward drag | 2 × 20 yd | 75s | Sink the hips, lean back, pull |
| RLE split squat | 3 × 6 each | 60s | Down the back knee, front knee tracks the toe |

*Cooldown (5 min): couch stretch, hamstring, calves.*

---

## DAY 4 — Lateral & Rotational Power + Upper / Total
*Warm-up: BASE + Change-of-Direction (full). NO MAX-V. Frontal/transverse-plane day — where the knee needs the most control. Stick and hold every landing.*

**JUMP — frontal + transverse**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lateral pogo prep | 2 × 10 each | 30s | Quick, stiff, side to side |
| Lateral hurdle hops, consecutive | 3 × 4 each | 60s | Stay low, quick off the ground |
| Lateral bound + stick | 3 × 4 each | 60s | Push wide, stick and hold — no knee cave |
| 90° rotational hop + stick | 3 × 4 each | 60s | Turn in the air, land balanced and quiet |
| SL multidirectional hops (clock, stick each) | 3 × 4 each | 75s | Hop to each point, stick every landing, knee stacked |

**AGILITY — reactive (keep it fun + competitive)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mirror drill (you lead, he reacts) | 4 × 20 sec | 40s | Read the hips, react — first step fast |
| Reactive cone cuts (you call / point) | 4–5 reps | 60s | Sharp plant, knee out, explode out of the cut |

**LIFT — Upper / Total (single-leg + iso finish)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| SL squat / pistol progression (to box) | 3 × 5 each | 75s | Control all the way down, knee tracks the toe |
| DB floor / bench press | 3 × 8 | 60s | Smooth and controlled both directions |
| Pull-up volume (optional 2nd dose) | 3 sets sub-max | 75s | Just bank quality reps |
| MB rotational throw | 3 × 6 each | 45s | Hips lead, finish through |
| Iso finisher: wall sit + RLE lunge iso hold | 2 × 25 sec each | 45s | Quiet and still, own the position |

*Cooldown (5 min): full-body flow + breathing.*

## The valgus fix — woven through, not bolted on
No separate corrective day. Knee-tracking lives in: the base warm-up (lateral lunge, fire
hydrants, hip circles); the CoD funnel (Days 3–4, directly targets foot/ankle, knee
stability, hip abduction); the stick on every landing ("knee out, over the 2nd–3rd toe,
freeze"); frontal-plane strength (Cossack, lateral lunge, RLE split squat, skater/lateral
bounds); single-leg bias; and the quality gate — STOP any set the moment knees cave.

## Testing & progress markers (re-test every 3–4 weeks)
10- & 20-yd dash (acceleration) · flying-10 (top-end) · broad jump (horizontal power) ·
SL hop distance each leg (SL power + valgus screen — watch side-to-side gaps) · drop-jump
landing screen (knee tracking under load). Frame as "beat your old number," never
comparison to others. Progress by adding; load creeps up only when technique is airtight.

## Tier note
This program is the **A/S-tier reference.** Lower tiers run the same skeleton with
regressed movements (double-leg landings, no depth drops, acceleration-only sprinting,
bodyweight/iso strength). See `Youth_Tiering_Assessment_Spec.md` §5.
