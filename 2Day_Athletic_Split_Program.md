# 2-Day Athletic Development Split — Reference Program

> A/S-tier reference program at 2-day frequency (Tue · Fri). Companion to
> `3Day_Athletic_Split_Program.md` and `4Day_Athletic_Split_Program.md`; same chassis, the
> minimum-effective-frequency version. Referenced by `BUILD_BRIEF.md` as a reference shape for
> assembled 2-day sessions. Governed by `COACHING_INSTRUCTIONS.md` (research-revised).

## How it works
Sprint / Jump / Lift chassis. Within every session: **Jump → Sprint → Lift** (highest-CNS
work first; strength closes). With only two sessions, **each day is total-body** — you can't
split upper/lower across a week, so both days cover the whole athlete, with a different speed
and power emphasis. This is the "if you only train twice" version: heavy in-season load,
limited access, or a busy multi-sport schedule.

**Hard rule:** max-velocity sprint quality is protected — never downstream of a HEAVY plyo
block. Day 1 (Tue) keeps the runs fresh with max-velocity; Day 2 (Fri) is acceleration +
change of direction with the higher plyo density and carries NO max-velocity. A small, sharp
plyo primer before the runs is fine.

**Sprint note (research-revised):** max-velocity uses FLYING-SPRINT structure — short build-up
into a short maximal fly zone — NOT long flat-out runs. Ramp high-speed volume ~5%/week.

**Two quality speed exposures, ≥48h apart:** Tue (max-V) and Fri (accel + CoD). The Tue/Fri
spacing is deliberate — it satisfies the ≤2 speed sessions/week, ≥48h-apart rule on its own.

### Weekly map
| Day | Session | Sprint Emphasis | Plyo Density | Warm-Up Funnel |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 (Tue) | Speed + Total-Body Strength (lower bias) | Max-V (fresh, flying) | Low (prime only) | Linear Speed |
| 2 (Fri) | Power / Multidirectional + Total-Body (upper + posterior bias) | Accel + CoD | High | Change of Direction |

Everything else is sport/rest. On any day with a game/practice, cut training volume ~30%
(likely relevant in-season). Sleep 9–10 hrs. Track height quarterly; ease dose through PHV.

## The Warm-Up System — Base + Funnel
Every session = shared BASE + day-specific FUNNEL.
**Funnel by day:** Day 1 → Linear Speed · Day 2 → Change of Direction.

### BASE LAYER — Daily Dynamic (every session, ~8 min)
**Locomotion (~10 yd each):** high-knee tuck · high-knee run · hurdler walk · butt-kick run ·
figure-4 walk · straight-leg march · walking lunge · reverse lunge · lateral lunge · dynamic
waiter's bow.
**Ground:** prisoner squats ×10 · overhead toe touch ×5/side · fire hydrants 5+5/side · hip
circles 5+5/side.

### FUNNEL A — Linear Speed (Day 1, full)
SL foot isolations → power skip for height → horizontal skip → A-skip → B-skip → power B-skip
→ straight-leg bounds. Keep skip/bound volume light — prime, don't pre-fatigue the runs.

### FUNNEL B — Change of Direction (Day 2, full)
Doubles as knee-tracking prep. Bilateral lateral shuffle → SL lateral movement → line hops →
lateral power shuffle → karaoke (power + speed) → backward run → backpedal. Knee tracks OUT
over the 2nd–3rd toe on every push/plant.

## DAY 1 — Speed + Total-Body Strength
*Warm-up: BASE + Linear Speed (full). Freshest day — max-velocity is the priority; jumps prime only.*

**JUMP — prime only (low volume; potentiates the runs)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Linear pogo | 2 × 10 | 30s | Stiff ankles, quick ground contact |
| SL pogo + stick | 2 × 5 / leg | 45s | Knee stacked over the toes, no cave |
| Low hurdle hop (2-foot) | 2 × 4 | 60s | Off the ground fast, soft knees |

**SPRINT — Max Velocity (flying-sprint structure)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Distance | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Build-ups (ramp to ~90%) | 3 | 30 yd | walk-back | Smooth acceleration, tall finish |
| Flying sprint (build → max fly @ 95–100%) | 4–5 | 20 yd build → 20 yd fly | ~3 min | All-out through the fly zone; run tall |

*End on quality drop-off. No 40–60 yd flat-out runs.*

**LIFT — Total-Body (lower bias)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Back squat (or trap-bar) | 4 × 4 | 2–3 min | Knees track out; controlled descent |
| RDL (DB / KB) | 3 × 6 | 90s | Hips back, flat back, feel the hamstrings |
| Bench / DB floor press | 3 × 6 | 90s | Smooth and controlled both ways |
| Neutral-grip pull-up (volume) | 4 × sub-max | 90s | Chase monthly total reps |

**TRUNK — rotation / anti-rotation**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest |
|---|---|---|
| MB rotational throw (wall) | 3 × 6 / side | 45s |
| Pallof press | 3 × 8 / side | 45s |

*Cooldown (5 min): hip flexor + hamstring + calf, slow breathing.*

## DAY 2 — Power / Multidirectional + Total-Body
*Warm-up: BASE + Change of Direction (full). NO max-velocity (downstream of the plyo block). Highest plyo density + CoD; includes motor-skill breadth.*

**JUMP — power density (contrast, all planes + SL)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Broad jump (non-consecutive, stick) | 3 × 3 | 75s | Jump far, land soft, knees out, freeze |
| Lateral bound + stick | 3 × 4 / side | 60s | Push wide, stick and hold — no cave |
| Hurdle hop → box jump (contrast) | 3 × (3 + 1) | 90s | Low-quick into one big explosion |
| SL bounds (consecutive) | 3 × 4 / leg | 90s | Big scissor, claw the ground back |

**SPRINT — Acceleration + Change of Direction (not max-V)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Distance | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Half-kneeling / 3-point start → sprint | 4 | 12–15 yd | 90s | Fire out flat and low |
| 5-10-5 shuttle | 3–4 | — | 2 min | Chop feet, sit hips, knee out on plant |
| Reactive cone cuts (coach calls) | 4 | — | 75s | React; sharp plant, explode out |

**MOTOR-SKILL — general movement breadth**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps / Time | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| MB overhead + rotational throw-catch | 3 × 6 / side | 45s | Power from the hips, turn through |
| Reactive ball-drop catch / coordination | 2–3 min | — | React fast, clean hands |

**LIFT — Total-Body (upper + posterior bias)**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Distance | Rest | Cue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Trap-bar deadlift (or heavy hinge) | 4 × 4 | — | 2–3 min | Flat back, drive the floor away |
| SL squat / split squat | 3 × 6 / leg | — | 90s | Control down, knee tracks the toe |
| 1-arm DB row | 3 × 8 / side | — | 75s | Elbow to hip, no twist |
| Nordic / buddy curl (control eccentric) | 3 × 5 | — | 90s | Fight gravity the whole way down |

**TRUNK — anti-rotation / anti-lateral**
| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Distance | Rest |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tall-kneeling Pallof press | 3 × 10 / side | — | 45s |
| Suitcase carry | 2 | 20 yd / side | 60s |

*Cooldown (5 min): couch stretch, hamstring, calves.*

## Design notes
- **Total-body both days** is the key difference from the 3- and 4-day splits — with two
  sessions there's no room to isolate upper/lower, so each day trains the whole athlete. Push
  and pull are spread across both days; posterior chain and single-leg appear both days.
- **Speed spacing:** Tue max-V and Fri accel/CoD are the two quality speed exposures, naturally
  ≥48h apart.
- **Hamstring load split:** max-V (Tue) and Nordics + heavy hinge (Fri) on separate days.
- **Valgus / landing woven in** the same way as the other splits: base hip work, CoD funnel,
  the stick on every landing, frontal-plane and single-leg strength. Stop any set on knee cave.
- **Trunk 2×/week** (one block each day), folded into the back of the lift.

## Testing & progress markers (re-test every 3–4 weeks)
10- & 20-yd dash · flying-10 · broad jump · SL hop distance each leg (also a valgus screen) ·
drop-jump landing screen. Frame as "beat your old number." Progress by adding quality; load
climbs only when technique is airtight.

## Tier note
This is the **A/S-tier reference.** Lower tiers run the same skeleton with regressed movements
(double-leg landings, no depth drops, acceleration-only sprinting, bodyweight/iso strength).
See `Youth_Tiering_Assessment_Spec.md` §5 and the assembler's tier gating.
